## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how **Call Tracker** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
*   **Account Information**: When you sign in via Google, we collect your email address and authentication ID to secure your account.
*   **User Content**: We collect the data you input into the app, including call logs, notes, contact names, and phone numbers.
*   **Files**: We store images or documents you upload as attachments to your call logs.

### B. Automatically Collected Information
*   **Device Information**: We may collect information about the device you use to access the app, such as the hardware model and operating system version.
*   **Usage Data**: We use Google Firebase services which may collect crash logs and performance events to help us improve the app stability.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
*   Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
*   Authenticate your identity and secure your data.
*   Sync your data across devices using cloud storage.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
*   **Firebase Authentication**: Managed by Google. Used for secure login.
*   **Cloud Firestore**: Your call logs are stored in a secure cloud database restricted by security rules that prevent unauthorized access.
*   **Firebase Storage**: Your attachments are stored securely and accessible only by you.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with third-party service providers (Google Firebase) strictly for the purpose of hosting and running the application infrastructure.

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to:
*   Access the personal information we hold about you.
*   Delete your account and all associated data directly within the application or by contacting support.
