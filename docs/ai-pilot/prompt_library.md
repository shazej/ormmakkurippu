# AI Prompt Library: Starter Kit (v1.0)

> [!TIP]
> Use these as templates! Replace the [Brackets] with your specific details.

---

## 🚀 For Sales & Marketing

### 1. The "Cold Outreach Polisher"
**Prompt**:
"You are an expert sales strategist. Rewrite the following cold outreach email [Paste Draft Below] to be more concise, value-driven, and friendly. Target Persona: [e.g., CTO of a 50-person startup]. Maintain a professional tone but avoid generic 'corporate speak.' 
**Constraint**: Keep the final email under 120 words."

### 2. The "Competitor Battlecard Creator"
**Prompt**:
"You are a market analyst. Based on publicly available information, compare [Our Product Name] with [Competitor Name]. Create a 5-point comparison table covering: pricing model, key features, and target audience. 
**Sandbox Rule**: Do not use any non-public pricing or confidential internal reviews."

---

## 💼 For HR & Operations

### 3. The "Job Description Generator"
**Prompt**:
"You are a technical recruiter. Draft a job description for a [Role Title, e.g., Senior Full-Stack Engineer]. Focus on these three key skills: [Skill 1], [Skill 2], and [Skill 3]. Our company culture is [Fast-paced/Creative]. 
**Constraint**: Use gender-neutral language and include a section on 'Why Join Us.'"

### 4. The "Internal Announcement Draft"
**Prompt**:
"Write a friendly internal announcement for our team about [Event/Policy Change]. Make it brief, highlight the key date ([Date]), and end with a call to action asking people to [Action]. Use a warm and supportive tone."

---

## 💻 For Engineering (Non-PII only)

### 5. The "Code Explainer"
**Prompt**:
"Explain the logic of this generic SQL query or open-source function in simple terms: [Paste Code Snippet]. Break it down into 3-5 bullet points. Why is this implementation efficient?"

### 6. The "Unit Test generator"
**Prompt**:
"You are a QA engineer. Write 5 edge-case unit tests using the Jest framework for a generic utility function that [Describe Function: e.g., validates email addresses]. 
**Constraint**: Do not include any proprietary internal modules; stick to standard JS types."

---

## ✍️ General Productivity

### 7. The "Meeting Transcript Summarizer"
**Prompt**:
"Below is a transcript of a recorded meeting [Paste Transcript - PUBLIC INFO ONLY]. Extract the top 3 action items, the primary decision made, and any unresolved questions. 
**Format**: Use a bulleted list for action items with [Owner] placeholders."
