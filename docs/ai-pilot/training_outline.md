# Training Outline: AI Lunch & Learn (60 min)

## Slide 1: Welcome & The "Why"
- **Goal**: Set a positive, curious tone.
- **Key Points**:
    - AI is an assistant, not a replacement.
    - We want to free you from "drudge work" (repetitive drafting, data cleanup).
    - This is a pilot: we are learning together.

## Slide 2: The Core Rule (Safety First)
- **Goal**: Highlight the Sandbox Data Policy.
- **Key Points**:
    - Review the "Red List" (No client data, no proprietary code).
    - Review the "Green List" (Public info, generic code, brainstorming).
    - *Action*: Show the [Sandbox Data Policy](sandbox_data_policy.md).

## Slide 3: What is a Large Language Model (LLM)?
- **Goal**: De-mystify the tech.
- **Key Points**:
    - Predictive text on steroids.
    - It predicts the *next* likely word, not "thinking."
    - Can hallucinate (state facts that aren't true).

## Slide 4: Elements of a Great Prompt
- **Goal**: Practical skill building.
- **Formula**: **Context + Task + Constraints + Format**.
- **Example**: "You are a senior copywriter [Context]. Summarize this public press release [Task] in 3 bullet points [Format]. Keep it professional and under 100 words [Constraints]."

## Slide 5: Use Case Demo - Non-Technical
- **Examples**:
    - Drafting a generic internal announcement.
    - Cleaning up a messy list of names (anonymized).
    - Brainstorming 10 headlines for a blog post.

## Slide 6: Use Case Demo - Technical
- **Examples**:
    - Writing a boilerplate unit test for a simple utility function.
    - Explaining a complex RegEx or SQL query.
    - Re-formatting JSON data.

## Slide 7: Human-in-the-Loop (Crucial)
- **Goal**: Validation.
- **Key Points**:
    - Never copy-paste without reading.
    - You are the subject matter expert; the AI is the intern.
    - Check for "too good to be true" stats or broken links.

## Slide 8: Logistics & Support
- **Goal**: Where to go next.
- **Key Points**:
    - How to get your seat login.
    - The #ai-pilot Slack channel for questions.
    - Weekly "Prompt Share" every Friday at 4 PM.

## Slide 10: Q&A
- Open floor for 10-15 minutes.
