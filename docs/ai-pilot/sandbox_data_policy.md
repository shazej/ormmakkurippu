# AI Usage Policy: Sandbox Phase (v1.0)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This policy is mandatory for all 25 pilot participants. Non-compliance may result in immediate suspension of pilot access.

## 1. The "Golden Rule"
**If the information is not publicly available on our website or in a press release, DO NOT input it into any AI tool.**

## 2. Forbidden Data (The "Red List")
Do **NOT** upload, paste, or describe the following to any AI tool:
- **Client Information**: Names, contracts, project details, or sensitive feedback.
- **Internal Financials**: Budgets, payroll, revenue, or specific product pricing.
- **Proprietary IP**: Source code (unless open source), unique methodologies, or trade secrets.
- **Personal Data (PII)**: Home addresses, phone numbers, or health information of staff or clients.

## 3. Permitted Data (The "Green List")
You **MAY** use AI for:
- **Public Communications**: Drafting blog posts, social media captions, or generic marketing copy.
- **Generic Coding**: Standard algorithms, UI styling, or boilerplate logic.
- **Administrative Tasks**: Rewording generic internal emails, summarizing public-domain articles.
- **Learning**: Asking for explanations of general concepts or software documentation.

## 4. Mandatory Settings
- **Chat History**: Ensure "Chat History & Training" is turned **OFF** in your settings if using a Personal/Free account.
- **Data Sharing**: Opt-out of "Share data to improve models" in the settings panel of [Tool Name].

## 5. What to do if a leak occurs
If you accidentally upload sensitive data:
1. **Delete** the specific chat session immediately.
2. **Notify** the Pilot Lead ([Name]) and IT within 1 hour.
3. **Document** what was shared so we can assess the risk.

---
**Acknowledgment**: By using the provided AI seats, you agree to adhere to these interim safety guidelines until formal legal clearance is finalized.
